Data Analysis on a consignment item from the HRO website.

John Q. Ham

John is 44 and works at Ham-Works. He has been saving for a number of years for a new to him radio. He has an old radio and is interested in getting a newer model. Since he has been saving for so long he wants a good return for his investment and the ability to do some of the newer modes of communication that have arisen in the last decade or so. He is not particularly 'stuck' with one brand, but does enjoy the features and operability of ICOM radios. He has researched various radios on the internet and has some idea of what he is looking for.

Use Case

John Q. Ham has done some research and is looking for an ICOM radio offering dual band features.

  • He enters the HRO website.
    • Site shows him the home page.
    • He clicks on the login.
    • He enters his username and password.
    • After login the site takes him back to the Home page.
  • He Chooses Products.
  • Site shows him a list of product types to choose from
  • He Chooses Consignment Items.
  • The site shows a page of items that are on consignment
  • He Scrolls through the listing and chooses: ICOM IC-2820H


Main Entities

store

  • storeId(primary key)
  • storeName
  • storeAddress
  • storeCity
  • storeState
  • storeZip
  • storePhone

user

  • userId(primary key)
  • userLastName
  • userFirstName
  • userAddress
  • userCity
  • userState
  • userZip
  • userEmail
  • userPhone
  • userSalt
  • userHash

Weak Entities

item

  • itemId(primary key)
  • itemDescription
  • itemPrice
  • itemMedia
  • userId(foreign key)
  • storeId(foreign key)

sale

  • saleId(primary key)
  • saleTimestamp
  • itemId(foreign key)
  • userId(foreign key)
  • storeId(foreign key)


Entity Interrelationship

  • A store can carry different consignment items from different customers. [1:many]
  • Customers can purchase one or more consignment items. [many:many]
  • A customer can have multiple items on consignment. [1:many]
  • A store can carry multiple consignment items from a customer. [1:many]
