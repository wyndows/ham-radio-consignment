Data Analysis on a consignment item from the HRO website.

John Q. Ham

John is 44 and works at Ham-Works. He has been saving for a number of years for a new to him radio. He has an old radio and is interested in getting a newer model. Since he has been saving for so long he wants a good return for his investment and the ability to do some of the newer modes of communication that have arisen in the last decade or so. He is not particularly 'stuck' with one brand, but does enjoy the features and operability of ICOM radios. He has researched various radios on the internet and has some idea of what he is looking for.

Use Case

John Q. Ham has done some research and is looking for an ICOM radio offering dual band features.

  • He enters the HRO website.
  • Chooses Products.
  • Chooses Consignment Items.
  • Scrolls through the listing and chooses: ICOM IC-2820H
